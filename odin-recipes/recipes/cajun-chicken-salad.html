Cajun Chicken Salad

A fruity, cajun infused salad with succulent textures for a perfect lunch.

Ingredients

  • 400g chicken fillets
  • Whole chopped pepper
  • Whole sliced orange
  • Whole diced cucumber
  • Bed of lettuce
  • Juice of half a lemon
  • Cajun spice powder
  • Balsamic vinegar
  • Olive oil

Method

  1. Add the chopped vegetables to the bed of lettuce
  2. Add the lemon juice, orange slices and balsamic vinegar
  3. Finish by adding the Cajun-fried chicken on top and serve
