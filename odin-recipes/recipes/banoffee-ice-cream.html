Banoffee Ice-Cream

A rich and indulgent combination of caramel and banana flavours, creating a might dessert for the big stomachs among us!

Ingredients

  • 2 large bananas
  • 2 tbsp Caranation Caramel
  • A handful of toasted, roughly chopped, pecan nuts
  • A cup of whipped cream
  • 2 Digestive biscuits
  • A cup of vanilla ice-cream

Method

  1. Line a chilled glass with some caramel
  2. Add the chopped nuts and digestives
  3. Add the banana, cream and ice-cream and serve
