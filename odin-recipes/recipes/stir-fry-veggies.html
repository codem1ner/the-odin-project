Stir-Fry Veggies

A fruity, cajun infused salad with succulent textures for a perfect lunch.

Ingredients

  • 200g broccoli
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 3 carrots (diced)
  • 1 can of water chestnuts
  • 200g mange tout
  • 1 bell pepper
  • Olive oil

Method

  1. Heat a pan with olive oil
  2. Fry the vegetables at a high heat for 3 mins.
  3. Serve
